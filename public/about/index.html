About

Hey, I'm Roland, nice to meet you! I'm a 31-year-old amateur photographer from Budapest, Hungary. I've been shooting on my phone a lot, and I picked up a digital camera just recently.

I regularly go out for photowalks around the city to hone my skills as a photographer. I spend a lot of time in post-production, practicing photo editing to come up with my own creative style.

I primarily shoot cars, landscapes and streets, but I don't shy away from the occasional stranger portraits every now and then.

When not out shooting or editing all those photos I take, I design webthings for a living, and I play video- and board games for fun.

This website contains some of my most favourite photos I took over the past few months trying to get to know to my camera. Please enjoy it, and don't be afraid to say hello!
