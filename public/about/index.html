About

Hey, I'm Roland, nice to meet you! I'm a 31-year-old aspiring amateur photographer from Budapest, Hungary. I've been shooting on my phone a lot, and I picked up a digital camera just recently.

I primarily shoot solo and couple portraits, vehicles, landscapes and streets, but I don't shy away from the occasional product and event photography every now and then.

I shoot on my trusty Sony Alpha a6000 with three of my favorite lenses: the Sigma 56mm f/1.4 and the Sony 85mm f/1.8 for automotive and portraits, and the Tamron 17-70 f/2.8 to run and gun. I also own a Samyang 16mm f/2.0 to do ultra-wide stuff.

When not out shooting or editing, I design webthings for a living, and I play video- and board games for fun.

Don't be afraid to say hello!
