Portrait Photo Walks

I do outdoor photo walks in Budapest with individuals, couples and families to create everlasting memories.

What is a Photo Walk?

It's a laid-back, no-pressure walking-talking-shooting experience with yours truly. We agree on a time and place to meet, and go from there.

How Does it Work?

  1. Preparation

    Before we get into things, you should have a basic idea for your photoshoot; a theme or a concept. Be sure to send me a few inspirational photos you found online. Here are a few examples to get you started:

    • Urban photoshoot with modern architectural backgrounds, wearing a smart outfit
    • Close-to-nature photoshoot, wearing a long white dress, located in one of the nearby nature reserves or forests in the afternoon
    • Night photoshoot downtown with lots of colorful bokeh balls in the background, wearing a shiny, reflective top to play with the city lights
    • Your creative idea goes here ðŸ˜‰

    Based on your preferences, we'll create a moodboard or a specific shot list together which we can refer to during your photoshoot.

    Please also have a ballpark number of photos you'd need, as we'll set the length of your photoshoot based on this.

  2. Photoshoot

    During the photoshoot I'll gladly assist you with prompts and posing, and tell a bunch of bad jokes to bring out your best smiles.

    I'll also want to listen to your story, if you're willing to tell me. I can also tell you my story, if you're interested.

    We don't need to strictly adhere to our moodboard, so if you find an interesting location that strikes your imagination, say it loud and we can take a detour. The best photos are the spontaneous ones!

  3. Delivery

    I'm a huge advocate of giving clients something on the day of the shoot. For the sake of this, you'll receive one of my favorite photos of the shoot for free, edited and retouched, at the end of the day.

    After this, I'll go through the photos we took and do the initial culling. This means I'll delete all the photos that are blurry, duplicate, or unbecoming. You don't need to see them, and I don't need to keep them.

    Once this is done, I'll send all the remaining photos for you to choose the ones you'd like me to edit and retouch. These photos will be in black-and-white, small size and watermarked. Please see the FAQ for more info.

    Once you come back to me with your selection, please allow me 1-3 days to finish editing and retouching your photos. You'll receive your final images digitally in a Google Drive folder (no email address required).

    If you need help printing your photos or creating a photo album, I can assist you with that as well.

Questions You Might Ask

If you have any more questions, don't hesitate to ask, I'll answer promptly!

Where do we walk?
We can walk and shoot wherever you want in Budapest. I prefer the inner districts for photographic possibilities, but I don't shy away from going to a nearby nature reserve or forest to take more close-to-nature photos. I'm available to shoot the entire day as well, however, in this case, a special pricing will be agreed upon.
How long is a photo walk?
It usually ranges from 30 minutes to 120 minutes or even more. I'm pretty versatile on time, so you can decide if you just want a few headshots taken or stay longer and visit multiple locations and even change outfits.
Do you edit the photos?
All photos you select after the shoot will be color-graded in a way that matches your personality, the location, and the theme of the shoot. Retouching includes removing skin imperfections and unnecessary flying hairs.
Will I get the raw photos, too?
I don't hand out unedited photos, only processed ones that reflect my personal editing style. This is to prevent people getting an inaccurate representation of the quality of my work.
Why are the raw photos black-and-white and watermarked?
The reason for B/W is so that you can better concentrate on the composition, your facial expressions and poses rather than the colors, which will be processed later anyways. Your photos for selection will be watermarked to denote that they're not final images.
Do you shoot in a studio as well?
Absolutely. Please reach out to me with your specific idea, and we'll figure out the best studio for your photoshoot. Pricing is the same for studio portraits, but please note that paying for the studio rental is your responsibility.
Do you do headshots?
Certainly. I can help you get professional headshots for LinkedIn, or your company's website or social media. Please note that for headshots, pricing is a little different. Don't hesitate to reach out to me for details, I'll be glad to assist you!
