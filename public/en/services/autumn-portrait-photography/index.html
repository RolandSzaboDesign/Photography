Pricing

The session fee of my portrait photography is 25.000 HUF, which contains 10× edited, retouched photos, which you yourself can choose after the photoshoot. You can select further photos for a price of 1.000 HUF each. Should you need many more photos, we'll figure out a personalized discount for you.

The photoshoot usually takes around 1-2 hours, but it can last for a maximum of 3 hours. Full-day (more than 3 hours) photoshoots are possible and welcome, for these, we'll figure out a personalized pricing structure based on your needs.

In certain cases, additional charges may occur:

  • If the place of your photoshoot requires me to use more than 1 mode of public transport, or it's unquestionably faster to drive (e.g. Normafa), I'll use a carsharing service to drive there on my own, and this fee should be paid by you. This is applicable to the outer districts of Budapest and the countryside. The maximum distance of the carsharing vehicles is ~100km in one direction, and their maximum daily fee is 14.000 HUF).
  • If your portrait shoot involves more than 1 location, I can help transport us between destinations safely, quickly and efficiently with carsharing. The carsharing fee should be paid by you, this is normally around a few thousand HUF in Budapest.
  • If you'd like to shoot at a place that requires an entry fee or a permit, these expenses should be paid by you.

Payment should be made after selecting your final photos for editing, and I've confirmed the number of photos. I accept bank transfer, Revolut, or PayPal. I do not accept cash. You will receive your final edited photos in 1-3 days after payment has been made.

You don't need to pay a retainer for your photoshoot.

Frequently Asked Questions

These are the same questions as with regular portrait photography

I'm not very photogenic, can you help me with posing?
Most of us aren't models who know how they'll look their best in photos. That's exactly how most people in my galleries felt before the photoshoot. Certainly, I'll help you with posing, but that doesn't mean your photos will look posed in the end. I prefer to capture candid moments, for which I'll give you some laid-back, fun, and sometimes silly prompts. These will be the photos with the purest emotions, which you'll love the most!
How would you describe your photography style?
My photography style is authentic and candid, with a bit of careful guidance and suggestions for portrait shoots. I'm more of the documenter/photojournalist type than the poser/director type. This means I prefer talking about a concept with you and just letting you be yourself, while I'm busy taking shots from all angles, capturing your most authentic moments. My editing style depends a lot on the time of day and time of year, but I love using warm, golden tones in my images, and I never over-edit my photos.
Where do we walk?
We can walk and shoot wherever you want in and around Budapest. You may choose any location that is close to your heart. If you don't have a specific idea, I can recommend the inner districts for their photographic possibilities, but I don't shy away from going to a nearby nature reserve or forest to take more close-to-nature photos.
How long is a photo walk?
We'll stay out for as long as you wish, up to 3 hours. If you just want a quick walk with a few shots, we're done in 30-60 minutes, but if you want to visit multiple locations and even change outfits along the way, we can stay out for 2-3 hours. If you'd like to shoot all day in multiple locations, that's all good for me, let's talk about it.
How will I get the finished photos after the shoot?
You'll receive your photos in a Google Drive or a Dropbox folder as a public link. This means that only those will be able to access the photos who have the link -- that's you and me only. This way, you don't need to give me your email address to view the photos. You'll be able to download the photos in their highest quality and resolution, to even be able to print them yourself.
How long do I need to wait for my edited photos?
Usually I'll send you the finished photos in 1-5 working days, after you've sent me the ones you selected for editing. Depending on how busy I am with shooting and editing at the given time, this interval might change, but I'll let you know about this well in advance.
What's your backup policy for your photos?
My camera has 2 identical memory cards in it, so every time I take a photo, it is written to both memory cards simultaneously. This way, in the unlikely event that one memory card fails, the other card will still have every single photo I took of you. At home, I copy the raw photos from my portrait shoots to two different hard drives, and I keep the backup files until I've sent you your final images.
Do you edit the final photos?
Every photo you receive will be individually edited and retouched. All photos you select after the shoot will be color-graded in a way that matches your personality, the location, and the theme of the shoot. Retouching includes removing skin imperfections and unnecessary flying hairs. Your photos won't be over-edited, they'll have just enough artistic touches applied to best tell your story. For retouching I use an artificial intelligence-driven editor, with which I can create shockingly beutiful results for you.
Will I get the raw photos, too?
It's very important for me that you only get 100% done, color-corrected images after your photoshoot. Whether you want to share them with your friends right away or send them to a lab for printing, your photos will be ready-for-use when you receive them. Therefore, I don't send out the unedited raw files.
Why are the photos in the proof gallery watermarked?
Your photos for selection will be watermarked to denote that they're not final images. Please do not share these raw images, as they're not the final products.
Are the final photos also watermarked?
No, they are not. I believe your final, edited photos speak for themselves and don't need watermarks.
Will my photos show up on your website?
It is important to me as a photographer to be able to share my latest and greatest work through my website, social media and advertisements. I usually select the best of the best images from my photoshoots, and upload them to my website. Before it happens, if you so prefer, I can send you the photos I've selected, so you can confirm that you like all of them. If you have any questions about this, definitely get in contact with me.
Can I share the final photos online?
Of course! You can use your photos for personal use in any way. If you give credit in the description, I'll thank you in a personalized comment. You can upload your photos to your own website, or to any social media platform, but you can print them out in virtually any size. The only thing you can not do is to send your photos to a company who would then use it in their own marketing.
Can I print the final photos?
Sure. You'll receive your photos in the range of 24 Megapixels in size, which is more than enough to print your photos in virtually any size.
Can I get some examples on pricing?
  • You really only need 5 photos — that's 25.000 HUF which is my current base fee. That will include an additional 5 photos for further use.
  • You need 10 photos — that's 25.000 HUF, same as the base fee.
  • You choose 20 photos after the shoot — that's a total of 35.000 HUF.
  • You need 12 photos and we used a carsharing service to travel between locations for 2.000 HUF — that's 25.000 (base fee) + 2.000 (+2 photos) + 2.000 (carsharing) = 29.000 HUF.
How many photos does your average client order?
My happy clients usually choose around 20 photos. Sometimes they only want 10 photos initially, but they quickly realize after the shoot that there are plenty more amazing shots they can choose from and usually go around or above 20 photos. The more photos you choose, the more discounts I can give you on your final order.
What kind of gear do you use?
I shoot with my trusty Sony Alpha 7 III professional full-frame mirrorless camera with a variety of top quality prime and zoom lenses. I've put together a separate page, if you want to learn more about my gear.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept bank transfer (preferred), Revolut, or PayPal. I don't accept cash payment. Please make the payment after you've sent me the selected photos for editing, and I've confirmed them with you. Thank you.
How can I book my shooting date?
You can write me an email here, through my website, or you can send me a Direct message over on Instagram. I don't necessarily prefer communicating through my Facebook Page, as I don't always get a notification there.
How far in advance should I book?
Usually I'm very flexible in terms of free time, so we can, in many cases, shoot on the day you write to me, or the day after. If you have an urgent photoshoot request, definitely contact me, so we can talk about when I can go walk-and-shoot with you. There's no extra charge for urgent photoshoots, if I can manage the shoot.
I'd like to shoot at night, is it possible?
Certainly, we can shoot in the evening hours with the city lights, or at night, even using ancillary light sources like my RGB lightsaber. It's important to mention that the preparation for these shoots usually take longer per location. Also, shots taken at night usually have more grain to them, due to the way camera sensors work. If you have a night project in mind, don't hesitate to reach out to me, I'd love to listen to your idea and talk to you more about the technicalities of what goes into shooting at night.
Can we shoot in bad weather as well?
Yes, all my equipment are weather-sealed, and my raincoat, even though I can fit in it twice, can't wait for me to wear it again.
Is it possible to shoot all day in multiple locations?
Absolutely. If you have this type of request, please let me know in advance, so I can plan my day ahead of time. These (3+ hours) photoshoots have an extra full-day commitment fee to them, which is determined on a case-by-case basis. For travelling between locations, I can drive you between places using one of the local e-carsharing services.
I'd like to shoot at a place where we'll need to be silent. Is it possible?
Yes, my camera has a silent mode feature where there's no shutter sound when a photo is taken. I can also walk on tippy-toes like an expert. If you like to shoot silently, let me know in advance!
Is it possible to shoot outside of Budapest, too?
Yes, absolutely. For these cases I use a local e-carsharing service, GreenGo, to get around. GreenGo employs only electric cars with a limited range, so it's best to stay in a 100 km vicinity of Budapest. The daily fee for using this service is 14.000 HUF; which you will need to pay for, on top of the base fee.
Do you also shoot events?
Definitely, I've shot public speeches, store openings, and even graduation ceremonies. Don't hesitate to reach out if you have an important event you'd like me to document, and we'll figure out all the details.
Do you shoot in a studio as well?
I'll only do studio sessions with couples or families. Please reach out to me with your specific idea, and we'll figure out the best studio for your photoshoot. Pricing is the same for studio portraits, but please note that renting and paying for the studio is your responsibility.
Do you do headshots?
Certainly. I can help you get professional headshots for LinkedIn, or your company's website or social media. Don't hesitate to reach out to me for details, I'll be glad to assist you!
I've lost the photos you sent me. What do I do now?
No worries, whatsoever. I've got all your photos on my hard drive and backed up in the cloud, so I can send them to you whenever, no fees attached. Drop me a line, and I'll send them over right away!
