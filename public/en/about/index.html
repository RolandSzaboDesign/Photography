About

Hey there, I'm Roland! At 34, I'm not just an aspiring amateur photographer from Budapest, Hungary—I'm someone who's passionate about capturing the genuine beauty in every moment.

I thrive on candid moments and the realness they convey. My aim is to bring out your authenticity and make you fall in love with portrait photography, just like I have.

For me, the camera is more than just a tool; it's an extension of my creative self, a means of communicating with people, and a constant companion on my journey. Photography has opened doors to connections that were previously unimaginable, and for that, I am eternally grateful.

When I'm not behind the lens or immersed in the world of editing, I'm designing websites for a living, indulging in video and board games, practicing cardistry, or trying to solve the 3√ó3 Rubik's cube faster than ever before.
