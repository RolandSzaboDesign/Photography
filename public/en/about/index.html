About

Hey, I'm Roland, nice to meet you! I'm a 33-year-old aspiring amateur photographer from Budapest, Hungary. I used to shoot on my phone a lot, and I picked up a digital camera some two years ago. I'd describe my work as authentic and spontaneous. I like capturing candid moments and showing legitimacy in my work.

I got very close to technology at a rather young age, and I've always been fascinated by the ways I was able to create. I've tried multiple avenues of creativity, including music and design, but photography has definitely become the one I got to feel the most fulfilled in.

The ability to capture authentic moments and share them with others is what drives me to wake up every day.

For me, the camera is not an object I only pick up and use from time to time. It's an extension of my creative self, it's my way of communicating with people. It's something I bring with me virtually everywhere I go. Photography has enabled me to connect with people that hasn't been possible before, and I'm forever grateful for it.

When not out shooting or editing, I design webthings for a living, and I play video- and board games for fun. My other hobbies are cardistry and getting quicker at solving the 3√ó3 Rubik's cube.

I'd love to hear from you, don't be afraid to say hello!

Some Interesting Facts

  • I'm an introvert. I absolutely love working with people and seeing them smile, but my social battery can sometimes drain rather quickly.
  • I'm a tech nerd. I love talking about technology and techniques in photography, so if you pop a question, be ready for the long answer.
  • I'm a perfectionist. I strive for the utmost precision and I fight for every single detail in my work; I can spend hours on the most minute details.
