Who is this for?

If you're feeling camera-shy or uncertain about your photogenic abilities, then this is the experience you've been waiting for! This introductory session is designed for new customers who want to dip their toes into the world of portrait photography. It's ideal for individuals who have never had a professional photo session or who may feel nervous in front of the camera.

This experience is only available in solo format. If you've already had the pleasure of shooting with me before, and want something more, I recommend upgrading to The Full Experience for a more immersive and personalized journey into the world of portraiture.

Who's Behind the Camera?

Great question! Hi, I'm Roland, and I help people like you feel your best in front of the camera. I firmly believe that everyone is inherently photogenic and I'm here to show it, one photograph at a time.

With my empathetic personality and horrible sense of humour, I'll create an easy-going atmosphere where you can be your true self and not be afraid of the camera. We talk a lot, we walk a lot and we laugh a lot together. We also take some photos here and there that you'll definitely love in the end.

You don't need to worry about posing, Ill be helping you all along the way. Now, I know what you're thinking: I don't want my photos to look posed. I get it! So instead of showing you concrete poses, I'll actually teach you how to look your best when you're being photographed, so you can feel more confident the next time it comes to taking your photos.
