This is my First Blog Post

Yeah, it's happening... finally!

A short backstory: I used to run a blog on web design when I was starting out, and it went for a good number of years. I loved the idea of having my thoughts put into words and that I could show them to the world.

On my new photography blog, you can expect short, helpful articles about photography, portraiture, posing, communication, and all kinds of geeky stuff.

I'm a very technical photographer and I love talking about it, so this Blog might very well become my playground for putting my thoughts into words.

Happy reading, and check back often! If you'd like me to write about a certain topic, feel free to let me know about it!
