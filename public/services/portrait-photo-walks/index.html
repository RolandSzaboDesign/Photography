Portrait Photo Walks

I do outdoor photo walks in Budapest with individuals, couples and families to create everlasting memories.

What is a Photo Walk?

It's a laid-back, no-pressure walking-talking-shooting experience with yours truly. We agree on a time and place to meet, and go from there. We can shoot more and talk less, or the other way around, it's up to you.

How Does it Work?

  • Preparation

    Before we get into things, you should have a basic idea for your photoshoot; what kind of photos you'd need. Be sure to send me any inspiration you like, including poses, facial expressions, places or environments, or just general concepts.

    Based on your preferences, we'll create a moodboard or a specific shot list that we can refer to during your photoshoot.

  • Photoshoot

    During the photoshoot I'll gladly assist you with location ideas, help you with posing and giving prompts, and tell a bunch of bad jokes.

    We don't need to strictly adhere to our moodboard, so if you find an interesting location that strikes your imagination, say it loud and we can take a detour.

  • Delivery

    I'm a huge advocate of giving clients something on the day of the shoot. For the sake of this, you'll receive one of my favorite photos of the shoot for free, edited and retouched, at the end of the day.

    After this, I'll go through the photos we took and do the initial culling. This means I'll delete all the photos that are blurry, duplicate, or unbecoming.

    Once this is done, I'll send all the remaining photos for you to choose the ones you'd like me to edit and retouch. These photos you initially get will be in black-and-white, small size and watermarked. The reason for B/W is so that you can better concentrate on the composition, your facial expressions and poses rather than the colors, which will be processed later anyways.

    Once you come back to me with your selection, please allow me 1-3 days to finish editing and retouching your photos. You'll receive your final images digitally in a Google Drive folder (no email address required).

Questions You Might Ask

Where do we walk?
We can walk and shoot wherever you want in Budapest. I prefer the inner districts for photographic possibilities, but I don't shy away from going to a nearby nature reserve or forest to take more close-to-nature photos.
How long is a photo walk?
It usually ranges from 30 minutes to 120 minutes or even more. I'm pretty versatile on time, so you can decide if you just want a few headshots taken or stay longer and visit multiple locations and even change outfits.
Do you edit the photos?
All photos you select after the shoot will be color-graded in a way that matches your personality, the location, and the theme of the shoot. Retouching includes removing skin imperfections and unnecessary flying hairs.
Will I get the raw photos, too?
I don't hand out unedited photos, only processed ones that reflect my personal editing style. This is to prevent people getting an inaccurate representation of the quality of my work.
Do you shoot in a studio as well?
Absolutely. Please reach out to me with your specific idea, and we'll figure out the best studio for your photoshoot. Pricing is the same for studio portraits, but please note that paying for the studio rental is your responsibility.
Do you do headshots?
Certainly. I can help you get a professional headshot for LinkedIn, or your company's website. Please note that for headshots, pricing is different. Don't hesitate to reach out to me for details!
Do you do TFCD/TFP shoots?
I'm actively looking for opportunities to broaden my skills in photography and diversify my portfolio with interesting subjects and concepts. If you have an idea in mind that you think I'll love, don't hesitate to get in touch with me, and we'll figure out a way to collaborate.
