Services

Portrait Photo Walks

I do outdoor photo walks in Budapest with individuals, couples and families to create everlasting memories.

What is a Photo Walk?

It's a laid-back, no-pressure walking-talking-shooting experience with yours truly. We agree on a time and place to meet, and go from there. We can shoot more and talk less, or the other way around, it's up to you.

During the photoshoot I'll gladly assist you with locations, posing and bad jokes.

Questions You Might Ask

Where do we walk?
We can walk and shoot wherever you want in Budapest. I prefer the inner districts for photographic possibilities, but I don't shy away from going to a nearby nature reserve or forest to take more close-to-nature photos.
How long is a photo walk?
It usually ranges from 30 minutes to 120 minutes or even more. I'm pretty versatile on time, so you can decide if you just want a few headshots taken or stay longer and visit multiple locations and even change outfits.
How much will it cost?
The photo walk and the shooting are free, you'll only need to pay for the images you select for editing and retouching afterwards.
How about editing and retouching?
All photos you select after the shoot will be color-graded in a way that matches your personality, the locations, and the theme of the shoot. Retouching includes removing skin imperfections and unnecessary flying hairs.
Will I get the raw photos, too?
I don't hand out unedited photos, only processed ones that reflect my personal editing style. This is to prevent people getting an inaccurate representation of the quality of my work.
