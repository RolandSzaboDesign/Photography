Portraiture

I love connecting with people through portrait photography.

I strive to create portrait photos that reflect the model's personality. During photoshoots I talk a lot to my models to get to know them better and portray them in a way that is the most true to themselves.

My favourite type of portraits is close-up headshots, where each little detail in the face adds to the whole story.
