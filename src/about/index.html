About

Hey, I'm Roland, nice to meet you! I'm a 32-year-old aspiring amateur photographer from Budapest, Hungary. I used to shoot on my phone a lot, and I picked up a digital camera about a year ago.

I primarily shoot solo and couple portraits, vehicles, landscapes and streets, but I don't shy away from the occasional product and event photography every now and then.

I shoot on my trusty Sony Alpha 6400 with a bunch of amazing lenses: the Sigma 56mm f/1.4, Sigma 30mm f/1.4, and the Sony 85mm f/1.8 as my primes, and the Tamron 17-70mm f/2.8 for run-and-gun situations.

When not out shooting or editing, I design webthings for a living, and I play video- and board games for fun. My other hobbies are cardistry and getting quicker at solving the 3×3 Rubik's cube.

Don't be afraid to say hello!
