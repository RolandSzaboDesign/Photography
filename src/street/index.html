Street

Ever since I picked up my camera, I've been regularly going out on photo walks in my city.

It's great practice to just go out and observe your surroundings for mundane things happening. Also, the long kilometers of walking keep me moderately fit.

I'm on an mission to stop taking the average "touristy" shots, and focus my images more around art and storytelling.
