Services

Portrait Photo Walks

I do outdoor photo walks in Budapest with individuals, couples and families to create everlasting memories.

What is a Photo Walk?

It's a no-pressure, laid-back, walking-talking-shooting experience with yours truly.

Questions You Might Ask

Where do we walk?
We can walk and shoot wherever you want in Budapest, although I prefer the inner districts for their own vibes and photographic possibilities.
How long is a photo walk?
It usually ranges from 30 minutes to 120 minutes or even more. This is good if you just want a few headshots taken or maybe even change outfits on longer shoots.
How much will it cost?
The photo walk and the shooting are free, you'll only need to pay for the images you select for editing afterwards.
How about editing and retouching?
All photos you select after the shoot will be color-graded in a way that matches your personality, the locations, and the theme of the shoot. Retouching includes removing skin imperfections and unnecessary flying hairs.
