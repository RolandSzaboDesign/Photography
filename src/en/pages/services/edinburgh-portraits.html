FAQs

How do I book a session?
Simply click the button below to book or send me a message. I’ll follow up with available times and details. Spots fill up quickly, so make sure to book early!
What if it rains?
No worries! I’m fully prepared to shoot in any weather, and rainy day portraits can actually look incredible. Let’s embrace the Scottish weather and have fun with it!
What should I wear?
Bring two outfits if you’d like to change during the shoot. I recommend wearing something that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
How many photos will I receive?
You’ll get 15 professionally edited photos, with the option to purchase additional ones for £5 each.
Can I bring someone with me?
Of course! If you’re looking for a couples shoot, feel free to bring your partner -- or your best friend for some fun portraits! Let me know in advance so I can plan accordingly.
What if my availability is limited?
Since I’m organizing multiple sessions on limited days, we’ll need to be a bit flexible with timing. But don’t worry—I’ll work with you to find a slot that works best and answer any concerns beforehand to make sure everything goes smoothly.
