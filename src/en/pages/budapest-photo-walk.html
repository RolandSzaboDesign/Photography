Budapest Photo Walk

Join us every month for a laid-back exploration of Budapest through our lenses! Whether you're a seasoned photographer, an up-coming enthusiast, or just someone who loves to capture moments with their phone, our photo walks are open to all. Entry is free.

  • Casual Atmosphere: No pressure, no hard competition – just a laid-back stroll through the city with fellow photography enthusiasts.
  • Diverse Locations: Explore different facets of Budapest's beauty on each walk. From historic landmarks to hidden gems, we'll cover them all. Eventually.
  • Networking: Connect with local photographers, models, and friends who share your passion for photography. We're all big talkers!

If you're interested in joining any of our photo walks in 2024, hit me with a DM on Instagram and I'll add you to our Group Chat, where I'll announce upcoming dates and themes. No spamming, promise!

Next Photo Walk Dates

25 February, Sunday, 17:00

Due to popular demand, the theme of the February Photo Walk is night photography. According to this, we're meeting at 17:00 local time, at our usual spot. If you want to do portraits, please bring external lights with you if you have any. If you'd like to do long exposure night photography, don't forget your tripod at home. We'll walk downtown, we'll be looking for interesting light sources, and we'll shoot street photography and do portraits (if we have models). I'd say we'll stay out until 20:00 local time, but of course if you need to go earlier, you can do so. If you haven't done night photography yet, it will be a great opportunity to learn from others; and if you're a seasoned veteran, you should come so you can help others learn. Let's meet at 5PM at Deák square, and let's experience our first 'night out' together at the Budapest Photo Walk!
