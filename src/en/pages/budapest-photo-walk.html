Budapest Photo Walk

Join us every month for a laid-back exploration of Budapest through our lenses! Whether you're a seasoned photographer, an up-coming enthusiast, or just someone who loves to capture moments with their phone, our photo walks are open to all. Entry is free.

What to expect:

  • Casual Atmosphere: No pressure, no hard competition – just a laid-back stroll through the city with fellow photography enthusiasts.
  • Diverse Locations: Explore different facets of Budapest's beauty on each walk. From historic landmarks to hidden gems, we'll cover them all. Eventually.
  • Networking: Connect with local photographers, models, and friends who share your passion for photography. We're all big talkers!

Next Photo Walk Dates

In January 2024 we're having two Photo Walks.

January 21st, 14:00

The regular Photo Walk will happen on the 21st of January. Meeting place is the usual Deák Ferenc square, next to The Ritz Carlton Hotel at 14:00 local time. No pre-determined destination, we'll figure something out based on the weather and lighting conditions. We might play a few light challenges along the way.

January 28th, 14:00

Our special Photo Walk this month will involve a group of 10 photo enthusiasts from Edinburgh. They're coming all the way to see our wonderful city and will be spending some time with us to go on a photo walk together. We have a pre-planned route, visiting Elizabeth Bridge, the Buda Castle and the Chain Bridge on the way back. We're meeting at 14:00 local time at Deák Ferenc square. English speaking is mandatory!
