Who's Behind the Camera?

Great question! Hi, I'm Roland, and I help people like you feel your best in front of the camera. I firmly believe that everyone is inherently photogenic and I'm here to show it, one photograph at a time.

On our photo sessions I strive to create a very authentic atmosphere where you can be your true self and not be afraid of the camera. We talk a lot, we walk a lot and we laugh a lot together. We also take some photos here and there. I achieve this intimate atmosphere with my horrible sense of humour and empathetic personality. You'll love it!

I'll also help you with posing. Now, I know what you're thinking: I don't want posed photos. Trust me, you do. I love capturing the in-between moments, but they have to be in between (get it?) poses. Instead of showing you poses, I'll teach you how to pose, so you can feel more confident next time you're being photographed.
